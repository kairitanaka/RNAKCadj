ssgsea_gsva_on_davids_signatures

This script runs ssgsea and GSVA on Davids signatures.

Including, making plots, and performing stats.

IIL TIL scores are also made.

requires data: 

'data/processed/DGE_final_samples.rda'
'data/raw/gene_signatures_with_APMs_IFNG.Symbol.txt'

creates data:

'data/processed/db_ssGSEA_David_correct.rda'
'data/processed/db_GSVA_David_correct.rda'
